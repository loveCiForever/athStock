**General Note on Model Structure**

The **News Analysis Model** processes Vietnamese news articles to identify stock tickers mentioned and predict their potential price impact. The structure is modular and flows through several stages:

1. **Input Handling**
   - Accepts a news article URL.
   - Scrapes and extracts the article's textual content.

2. **Preprocessing**
   - Cleans the extracted text (e.g., removes HTML tags, special characters).
   - Tokenizes and standardizes text for Vietnamese language processing.

3. **Stock Ticker Detection**
   - Scans the cleaned text for mentions of stock codes.
   - Matches against a list of valid stock tickers from HOSE, HNX, or UPCOM.

4. **Sentiment and Impact Analysis**
   - Applies a pretrained deep learning model (e.g., PhoBERT) to analyze sentiment.
   - Maps sentiment to stock movement categories (e.g., "increase strongly", "decrease slightly", "neutral").

5. **Output Generation**
   - For each detected stock ticker, generates a natural language prediction.
   - Example output: "News likely causes VCB to increase strongly."
   - Supports multiple stock codes per article.

6. **Model Enhancements (Optional/Future Work)**
   - Use of Named Entity Recognition (NER) for better context detection.
   - Fine-tuning on domain-specific financial data for improved accuracy.

**Simple Flow Diagram:**

[Input URL] -> [Scrape Article Text] -> [Preprocessing] -> [Detect Stock Tickers] -> [Sentiment/Impact Prediction] -> [Generate Output]

---

This modular structure allows easy upgrades and improvements for higher accuracy and broader stock market insights.
